# 🥗 Crave

### *Your AI-powered nutrition, fitness, and wellness companion.*

NutriFit AI is an intelligent wellness platform that combines nutrition tracking, personalized fitness planning, AI-powered meal recommendations, and community engagement into a single application.

Built with modern AI technologies, the platform helps users make healthier lifestyle choices by recommending recipes, tracking nutrition, suggesting nearby healthy restaurants, and generating personalized workout plans based on dietary preferences, fitness goals, and cultural requirements.

### Faith-Aware & Balanced Fitness Goals

NutriFit AI supports culturally and religiously inclusive fitness planning.

Examples include:

* Fasting-aware workout schedules
* Ramadan meal timing adjustments
* Vegetarian and vegan fitness plans
* Jain-friendly nutrition guidance
* Protein alternatives
* Flexible workout intensity during fasting periods

The goal is to encourage sustainable health while respecting personal beliefs and dietary practices.
